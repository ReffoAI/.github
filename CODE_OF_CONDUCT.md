**Code of Conduct**

**Our Commitment**
Reffo is building open infrastructure for decentralized commerce. We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone — regardless of experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, appearance, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.

**Expected Behavior**
Be respectful and constructive in discussions, issues, and pull requests
Give and receive feedback gracefully
Assume good intent — ask for clarification before assuming the worst
Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
Focus on what is best for the community and the protocol

**Unacceptable Behavior**
Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
Publishing others' private information without consent
Sustained disruption of discussions or contributions
Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

**Scope**
This Code of Conduct applies to all Reffo project spaces — including GitHub repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, and any official communication channels. It also applies when representing the project in public spaces.

**Enforcement**
Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to conduct@reffo.ai. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially.
Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban from the project.

**Enforcement Guidelines**
Correction — A private written warning with clarity on the violation. A public apology may be requested.
Warning — A formal warning with consequences for continued behavior. No interaction with involved parties for a specified period.
Temporary Ban — Temporary removal from all project spaces.
Permanent Ban — Permanent removal from all project spaces.
